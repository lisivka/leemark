About me

Hi I'm Svitlana Lee Mark

My name is Svitlana Lisivka Markovets (Svitlana Lee Mark). I live and work in Ukraine. I'm safe now.
I paint in different techniques, sometimes completely opposite. Realism, abstraction, landscape, animals- I’m interested in trying everything.
But most of all I like to paint with oil in my author’s technique - as I call it "Network". These works are as if woven with a brush and imitate weaving and netting. The creation of each such work requires many stages and a lot of time. It is very difficult to choose an object or plot. Therefore, each of them is unique and inimitable.
At each exhibition, it is near these works that the audience stops and takes a long time to examine them. There is always a little fairy tale and magic in these works.

You can see it for yourself. Watch the video from my exhibition on facebook.com
